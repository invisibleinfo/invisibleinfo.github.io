# Invisible Films

Addresses systemic obscurity faced by cinematic works from under-represented regions, languages, and communities hidden beyond algorithmically curated content streams. The project maps global film production and distribution data to reveal which cinematic traditions are invisible to mainstream audiences and streaming platforms.

Project in progress, reach out to [s.khanna@uva.nl](mailto:s.khanna@uva.nl) for further details.
