# Invisible Perceptions

Investigates and quantifies the invisibility of human perceptions and judgments, revealing biases propagated through visual media. Using image-based experiments via our open-source tool _Pictopercept_, the project studies how people perceive and evaluate visual information, including how those perceptions differ across cultures, demographics, and media contexts.
