# Invisible Books

Finding literary gems obscured by mainstream publishing and digital ranking systems, spotlighting authors and works marginalized by linguistic barriers, geographic isolation, or cultural factors. The project analyzes global book metadata and obscure reader reviews to surface overlooked literature from under-represented traditions.

Project in progress, reach out to [s.khanna@uva.nl](mailto:s.khanna@uva.nl) for further details.
