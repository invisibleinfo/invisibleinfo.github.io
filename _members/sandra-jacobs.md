Sandra Jacobs (PhD, Utrecht University, School of Governance) is Assistant Professor in Corporate Communication at the University of Amsterdam, Amsterdam School of Communication Research (ASCoR). In her research, she focuses on strategic communication of organizations, mediatization, and the mediated construction of societal debates.
