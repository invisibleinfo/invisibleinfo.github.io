Huiwen (Iria) Cheng is a Research Master student at the University of Amsterdam. She completed her bachelor's degree in Journalism, motivated by a passion for promoting social justice. During her Master's program at UvA, Cheng gained experience in both quantitative and qualitative research and is advancing her computational communication skills. Her interests lie in social issues, especially those surrounding gender (in)equality.
