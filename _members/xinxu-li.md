Xinxu Li majored in Communication Science (Corporate Communication) at University of Amsterdam, with a background of English (Culture and Communication) studies at Guangdong University of Foreign Studies in China. Her research interests focus on cross-cultural leadership communication, intersectionality in communication, and strategic communication in organizational settings.
