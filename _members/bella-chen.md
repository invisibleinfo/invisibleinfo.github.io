Bella Chen is a Research Master student in Communication Science at the University of Amsterdam, Amsterdam School of Communication Research (ASCoR). Her research interests focus on biodiversity communication, public perception, and conservation support. Her work also involves developing PictoPercept, an open-source toolkit for studying image-based perception and decision-making.
