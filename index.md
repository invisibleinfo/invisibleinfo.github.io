The Invisible Information Lab is a research group at the Amsterdam School of Communication Research (ASCoR), University of Amsterdam. We study knowledge that is missing, marginalized, or invisible in our increasingly digitized lives - and what that means for fairness, equity, and the nature of human knowledge. Our work bridges the gap between state-of-the-art computational methods and community-driven research initiatives, ultimately to make the invisible visible and actionable for a more informed and inclusive society.
