We are a research group at the Amsterdam School of Communication Research, University of Amsterdam. We study knowledge that is missing or marginalized - and hence invisible - in our increasingly digitized lives, including what this invisibility means for fairness, equity, and the nature of human knowledge. Our work spans modern computational methods and community-driven research initiatives, ultimately to make the invisible visible and actionable for a more informed and inclusive society.
